Divisions & Roles

Become a contributor in a division of Labradoria, not just a citizen with a title

Why Divisions Matter

Labradoria citizenship isn't about receiving a title. It's about contributing meaningfully to a nation that actually functions. We organize contributors into three operational divisions where your skills create real value for our community. Each division has clear roles, expectations, and opportunities for advancement.

⚙

Technology Division

Infrastructure, Development, Systems

Build and maintain the digital infrastructure that powers Labradoria. This division oversees our hosting, citizen platforms, security systems, and technological innovation.

Potential Roles:
  • Systems Engineer
  • Developer / Full-Stack Engineer
  • Security Specialist
  • DevOps / Infrastructure
  • Database Administrator
  • QA / Testing Specialist

Managed by the Minister of Science, Technology & Innovation. Partners with CookieHost.uk for infrastructure delivery.

Join Tech Division
📢

Media Division

Communications, Content, Outreach

Tell Labradoria's story to the world. This division creates content, manages communications, handles press relations, and builds our public presence across platforms.

Potential Roles:
  • Press Officer
  • Content Creator / Writer
  • Social Media Manager
  • Designer / Graphic Artist
  • Videographer / Multimedia Producer
  • Communications Strategist

Reports to the President's Office. Operates the Press Office and manages diplomatic communications.

Join Media Division
🔍

Intelligence & Research

Policy, Analysis, Knowledge

Conduct research and analysis that informs Labradoria's policies. This division studies international law, emerging technologies, governance best practices, and trends affecting our nation.

Potential Roles:
  • Policy Analyst
  • International Law Researcher
  • Technology Futures Analyst
  • Data Analyst
  • Governance Specialist
  • Think Tank Researcher

Partners with the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Produces white papers, policy recommendations, and strategic analysis.

How to Participate

1. Choose Your Division: Select the division that aligns with your skills and interests. You can indicate preferences during your citizenship application or join an active division after citizenship approval.

2. Learn Our Systems: Upon joining a division, you'll receive training on Labradoria's infrastructure, values, and operational procedures—including access to internal systems and communication platforms.

3. Make Meaningful Contributions: Work on real projects that benefit the nation. Technology division members maintain our hosting; Media division creates our communications; Research division produces policy papers.

4. Advance Your Role: Demonstrate competence and commitment to advance within your division. Leadership positions carry greater responsibility and recognition from the Cabinet.
