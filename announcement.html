Presidential Declaration on the Recognition of Sovereign States

Issued by: The President's Office, Republic of Labradoria  ·  Date: 3 April 2026

The President of the Republic of Labradoria, Freddie Sparrow, acting in his capacity as Head of State and Head of Government of the Republic of Labradoria, and pursuant to the authority vested in him by the Constitution of the Republic, hereby issues the following formal declaration for the attention of all foreign governments, diplomatic missions, and international bodies.

I. Recognition of United Nations Member States

The Republic of Labradoria hereby formally, officially, and unconditionally recognises all current member states of the United Nations as sovereign nations under international law. The Republic extends to each and every such state its goodwill, respect for their sovereignty and territorial integrity, and its openness to diplomatic engagement, mutual recognition, and lawful cooperation.

II. Recognition of Additional Sovereign Entities

In addition to the foregoing, the Republic of Labradoria hereby extends formal recognition of sovereignty to the following nations and entities, which, whilst not members of the United Nations, are recognised by this Republic as sovereign:

  • The Free Republic of Verdis
  • The Principality of Sealand
  • The Republic of Ironland

The Republic of Labradoria invites each of the above-named entities to engage in diplomatic dialogue and, where mutually agreeable, to enter into formal instruments of mutual recognition with the Republic.

III. Peaceful & Non-Aggressive Posture

The Republic reaffirms, for the benefit of all foreign governments and international bodies, that Labradoria is an entirely peaceful, unarmed, and non-aggressive nation. The Republic maintains no military forces, holds no weapons, and formally declares that it shall never acquire, develop, or possess nuclear, chemical, biological, radiological, or any other weapons of mass destruction. Any dispute involving the Republic shall be resolved exclusively through diplomatic channels and peaceful mechanisms.

IV. Invitation to Diplomatic Engagement

The Republic of Labradoria extends a standing invitation to all recognised states and sovereign entities to open diplomatic dialogue. The Republic is open to mutual recognition agreements, treaty negotiations, and all forms of peaceful international cooperation.

Freddie Sparrow President of the Republic of Labradoria
Head of State & Head of Government
