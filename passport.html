Labradoian Passport

Official travel document of the Republic of Labradoria  ·  £100 application deposit

About the Document

What is a Labradoian Passport?

A Labradoian passport is the official travel document and primary identity document of the Republic of Labradoria, issued by the Ministry of Internal Affairs & Citizenship to all citizens in good standing. It bears the coat of arms of the Republic, the citizen's personal details, and constitutes formal proof of Labradoian citizenship.

The passport is issued under the authority of Article XIV of the Constitution of the Republic of Labradoria.

Validity

Valid for 3 years from the date of issue. Must be renewed before or upon expiry to remain in good standing.

Who Can Apply

Any citizen of the Republic in good standing. You must have confirmed citizenship before applying for a passport.

Official Document

Bears the Republic's seal and coat of arms. Constitutes formal proof of Labradoian citizenship and national identity.

Issued By

Ministry of Internal Affairs & Citizenship, Republic of Labradoria.

Important Legal Notices

Non-recognition: Labradoian passports are not currently recognised by third-party states for the purposes of international travel. You must continue to use travel documents issued by your host nation for all international travel. A Labradoian passport does not confer any right of abode, residence, entry, or work in any country. The Republic is actively pursuing diplomatic recognition of its travel documents through bilateral channels.

Shipping & Delivery: Passports are a physical document and will be posted to your registered address. Shipping and postage costs are not included in the application deposit and will be charged separately. The applicable shipping cost will be confirmed at the time of issuance. The Republic is not responsible for delays or losses caused by postal services.
Fee Schedule

Fees & Payments

Current Fee Schedule

New passport application deposit (non-refundable) £100
Passport renewal fee £60
All fees are non-refundable once submitted, including in the event of refusal or withdrawal. Fees are held in the Treasury and applied to administrative costs of the Republic. Fee schedule subject to revision with 30 days' notice.

Payment details will be provided upon receipt of your application. All payments are processed securely. No funds are applied to private benefit.

Application Process

How to Apply

1

Confirm your citizenship

You must be a confirmed citizen of the Republic of Labradoria in good standing before applying. If you are not yet a citizen, apply for citizenship first.

2

Submit your application

Contact the Ministry of Internal Affairs & Citizenship via the button below. Include your full legal name, date of birth, citizenship reference (if known), and a brief statement confirming your request for a passport.

3

Pay the application deposit

Upon receiving your application, the Ministry will provide secure payment details for the £100 non-refundable deposit. Your application will be processed once payment is confirmed.

4

Verification & issuance

The Ministry will verify your citizenship status and details. Upon successful verification, your passport will be prepared and delivered to you. You will be notified at each stage.

5

Renewal

Passports are valid for 3 years. Contact the Ministry before expiry to renew. The renewal fee is £60. The £100 deposit is not charged on renewals.

Legal Framework

Constitutional Basis

Labradoian passports are issued under Article XIV of the Constitution of the Republic of Labradoria, which establishes the Republic's authority to issue passports and identity documents, sets out the fee structure, and provides the legal framework governing their issuance, validity, and revocation.

All passport decisions are subject to the safeguards of Article X (Citizenship) and Article XXIII (Supremacy & Constitutional Review). A passport may be refused or revoked if citizenship is revoked, or where the President and Cabinet determine issuance is contrary to the interests of the Republic.
