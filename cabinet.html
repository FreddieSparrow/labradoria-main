Executive Branch

Cabinet of Ministers

The Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Labradoria constitutes the primary executive council of the Government, responsible for the formulation and implementation of national policy across all ministerial portfolios. Cabinet Ministers are appointed by and serve at the pleasure of the President of the Republic, in accordance with the provisions of the Constitution.
