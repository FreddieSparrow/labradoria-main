3 April 2026 — President's Office

Presidential Declaration: Recognition of States

President Freddie Sparrow formally and officially recognises all UN member states, plus the Free Republic of Verdis, the Principality of Sealand, and the Republic of Ironland as sovereign nations.

2 April 2026 — Ministry of Foreign Affairs

Territorial Claims Formally Published

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs has published the official register of Labradoian territorial claims, including Bir Tawil, Marie Byrd Land, and voluntarily ceded private parcels worldwide.

View territorial register →
1 April 2026 — Ministry of Foreign Affairs

Cession Programme Open to All Nations

The Republic now accepts voluntary Instruments of Cession for privately owned land in any country. Some private parcels, such as those in France and the United Kingdom, are held in confidence — only governments may request details of such parcels for privacy reasons.

1 April 2026 — Constitutional Assembly

Constitution Ratified

The founding Constitution of the Republic of Labradoria has been ratified, enshrining the rights of all citizens, the dispute resolution framework, and the structure of the national government.
