Land Cession Programme Open to All Nations

The Republic now accepts voluntary Instruments of Cession for privately owned land in any country, subject to jurisdictional requirements. Western liberal democracies: cessions accepted directly. Presidential Office

Presidential Statements

Statement — 3 April 2026 — President Freddie Sparrow

The Republic of Labradoria is, and shall always remain, a peaceful and unarmed nation. We recognise the dignity of every human person and the sovereignty of every nation. We invite the world to see us not as a novelty but as a sincere effort to build a community guided by law, fairness, and shared values.

We hold no weapons. We threaten no one. We ask only to exist alongside all nations in peace, and to be recognised for what we are: a genuine, law-abiding, and principled sovereign community.

Freddie Sparrow  ·  President, Republic of Labradoria
