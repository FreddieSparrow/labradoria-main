State Finances

A transparent record of all expenditure and income of the Republic of Labradoria.

Commitment to Transparency

Our Financial Principles

The Republic of Labradoria is committed to full financial transparency. All state expenditure — however small — is recorded and published here. Income from citizenship fees, document fees, and donations is also recorded. The Republic currently operates without taxation; all costs are met from fees and voluntary donations.

Note: All figures are approximate where marked (~). The Republic does not currently hold a dedicated state bank account; transactions are processed through designated personal accounts used exclusively for Labradorian purposes, identifiable by the LABRADORIA payment reference system.
