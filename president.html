President Freddie Sparrow serves as the founding President of the Republic of Labradoria, having assumed office upon the ratification of the Constitution in 2026. As Head of State and Head of Government, President Sparrow holds executive authority over all matters of national governance, foreign affairs, and territorial administration.

Under the Constitution of the Republic, the President serves a term of five years, with the option of renewal subject to the procedures established therein. President Sparrow is the first to hold this office and is responsible for the establishment of all foundational institutions of the Republic.

Presidential Oath of Office

"I, Freddie Sparrow, do solemnly affirm that I will faithfully execute the Office of President of the Republic of Labradoria, and will to the best of my ability, preserve, protect, and uphold the Constitution and the sovereign interests of the Republic and its people."
