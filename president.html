Biography

About the President

President Freddie Sparrow serves as the founding President of the Republic of Labradoria, having assumed office upon the ratification of the Constitution in 2026. As Head of State and Head of Government, President Sparrow holds executive authority over all matters of national governance, foreign affairs, and territorial administration of the Republic.

Under the Constitution of the Republic, the President serves a renewable term of five (5) years. President Sparrow is the first to hold this office and is responsible for the establishment of all foundational institutions of the Republic, including the Cabinet of Ministers, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Territorial Affairs, and the formal publication of the Labradoian Territorial Register.

Presidential Oath of Office
"I, Freddie Sparrow, do solemnly affirm that I will faithfully execute the Office of President of the Republic of Labradoria, and will to the best of my ability, preserve, protect, and uphold the Constitution and the sovereign interests of the Republic and its people."
Presidential Declaration

Recognition of States — 3 April 2026

The President of the Republic of Labradoria hereby formally and officially recognises all member states of the United Nations as sovereign nations. The Republic additionally extends formal recognition to the Free Republic of Verdis, the Principality of Sealand, and the Republic of Ironland. The Republic of Labradoria invites all recognised states to engage in diplomatic dialogue and mutual cooperation.
