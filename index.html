Welcome to the Republic of Labradoria

Sovereign Micronation  ·  Founded on Freedom, Innovation & Communal Governance

The Republic of Labradoria is a sovereign micronation dedicated to the principles of freedom, innovation, and communal self-governance. Our republic is built upon the consent of its citizens and the voluntary participation of all who wish to join our growing national community. Our official symbols embody the values of loyalty, strength, and visionary leadership.

Labrador Retriever
Symbolizing loyalty and companionship.

Bald Eagle
Symbolizing freedom and sharp vision.

Territorial Claims Formally Published

Cession Programme Open to All Nations

The Republic now accepts voluntary Instruments of Cession for privately owned land in any country. Contact the Ministry to begin the registration process.

Constitution Ratified

The founding constitution of the Republic of Labradoria has been ratified, enshrining the rights of all citizens and the structure of the national government.
