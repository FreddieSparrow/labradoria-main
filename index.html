Official Notice to All Foreign Governments, Diplomatic Missions & International Bodies

The Republic of Labradoria is an entirely peaceful and unarmed sovereign nation. The Republic maintains no military forces, holds no weapons of any kind, and bears no hostile intent toward any state, government, or people. Labradoria is a proudly Christian nation, guided at all times by the principles of peace, charity, and the inherent dignity of the human person. The Republic hereby formally declares and affirms that it does not now possess, nor does it intend at any future time to acquire, develop, produce, stockpile, or otherwise obtain nuclear, chemical, biological, radiological, or any other weapons of mass destruction. In the event that the Republic is subject to any act of aggression, threat, coercion, or hostility by any party whatsoever, the Republic shall respond exclusively through diplomatic channels, recognised international legal mechanisms, and peaceful arbitration — the use of force or violence shall never be countenanced as a response under any circumstances. The Republic invites all nations and international bodies to engage with Labradoria in a spirit of friendship, mutual recognition, and lawful cooperation.

About the Republic

What is the Republic of Labradoria?

Labradoria is a peacefully constituted sovereign micronation established in 2024 and formally constituted in 2026. We are a nation built upon law, faith, and the voluntary participation of our citizens and allies.

✓

Peaceful & Unarmed

Labradoria maintains no military forces and holds no weapons of any kind. We are formally committed to the peaceful resolution of all disputes through diplomacy and international law.

✦

Christian Nation

The Republic is proudly Christian, guided by the principles of Christian charity, the common good, and the dignity of every human person, in accordance with Christian values and teachings.

⚖

Rule of Law

All organs of the Republic operate under a written Constitution. Citizens' rights are enshrined and protected. All territorial declarations comply with applicable international norms.

●

Open to the World

Any individual or landowner in any country may join the Republic or voluntarily cede privately owned land to Labradoria. We welcome all who share our values of peace and community.

Ministry of Foreign Affairs & Territorial Affairs

Territorial Claims of the Republic

The Republic of Labradoria formally asserts sovereign interest over the following territories pursuant to applicable principles of international customary law. All declared lands situated within the jurisdiction of a recognised host state remain fully subject to that state's domestic law. Additional detailed policy documents and links to these territories will be published upon approval of the Cabinet by a vote of at least fifty-five percent (55%), in accordance with Article XXI of the Constitution.

I
Unclaimed Land — Antarctica

Marie Byrd Land, Antarctica

The sole unclaimed sector of the Antarctic continent (~1.6 million km²). Labradoria asserts declaratory sovereignty while affirming its commitment to the peaceful and scientific purposes of the Antarctic Treaty System.

II
Maritime Interest — High Seas

International Waters & High Seas

The Republic asserts its rights as a sovereign state to flag vessels and operate under its jurisdiction on the high seas in accordance with UNCLOS Articles 86–120. No territorial sovereignty over international waters is claimed.

III
Voluntarily Ceded — Any Jurisdiction Worldwide

Voluntarily Ceded Private Land — All Nations

Any individual or legal entity holding valid title to privately owned real property under the domestic law of any country in the world may voluntarily cede that property to Labradoria via a formal Instrument of Cession. Such parcels are incorporated into the Labradorian Territorial Register. All ceded lands remain fully subject to host-nation domestic law.

Presidential Term — 5 Years

The President of the Republic serves a term of five (5) years, renewable in accordance with the Constitution. The current term runs from 2026 to 2031. President: Freddie Sparrow.
