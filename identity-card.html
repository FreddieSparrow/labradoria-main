About the Document

What is the National Identity Card?

The Labradoian National Identity Card is an official document issued by the Ministry of Internal Affairs & Citizenship. It is a compact, wallet-sized card bearing the citizen's name, citizenship number, and the seal of the Republic — serving as formal proof of Labradoian citizenship.

It is the most accessible Labradoian identity document, priced at £30 — lower than the passport — and is suitable for citizens who wish to carry proof of their Labradoian nationality without applying for a full passport.

Validity

Who Can Apply

Any confirmed citizen of the Republic in good standing. You must hold citizenship before applying.

Not a Travel Document

The identity card is not a travel document. It cannot be used in place of a host-nation passport for international travel.

Important Legal Notices

Non-recognition: The Labradoian National Identity Card is not recognised by third-party states and cannot be used as a form of identification for any purpose in host nations. It does not confer any right of abode, residence, entry, employment, or benefits in any country. Citizens must continue to use identity documents issued by their host nation for all host-nation purposes.

Shipping & Delivery: Identity cards are a physical document and will be posted to your registered address. Shipping and postage costs are not included in the £30 fee and will be charged separately. The applicable shipping cost will be confirmed at the time of issuance. The Republic is not responsible for delays or losses caused by postal services.

Application Process

How to Apply

1

Confirm your citizenship

You must be a confirmed citizen of the Republic in good standing. Not yet a citizen? Apply for citizenship first.

2

Submit your application

Contact the Ministry of Internal Affairs & Citizenship via the button below. Include your full legal name, date of birth, and citizenship reference number (if known). Specify that you are applying for a National Identity Card.

3

Pay the application fee

The Ministry will provide secure payment details for the £30 non-refundable fee. Your application is processed once payment is confirmed.

4

Verification & issuance

The Ministry verifies your citizenship and details. Upon successful verification, your identity card is prepared and delivered. You will be kept informed at each stage.

5

Renewal

Identity cards are valid for 2 years. Contact the Ministry before expiry. Renewal fee is £20.

Legal Framework

Constitutional Basis

Labradoian identity cards are issued under Article XIV of the Constitution of the Republic of Labradoria, which grants the Republic authority to issue passports, identity cards, and travel documents, sets out the fee structure, and establishes the legal framework for issuance, validity, and revocation.

Identity cards may be refused or revoked if a citizen's citizenship is revoked under Article X, or where the President and Cabinet determine that issuance would be contrary to the interests of the Republic.
