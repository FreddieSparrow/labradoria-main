How to Apply

Applying for Citizenship

Citizenship of the Republic of Labradoria is open to anyone who affirms the Constitution and shares the values of the Republic. The process is simple and conducted entirely by email.

🚫 Citizenship Bans — The Republic maintains a confidential register of individuals who are permanently or temporarily prohibited from obtaining citizenship. If you wish to establish whether you appear on this register, you may enquire by email to office@labradoria.org with the subject line Citizenship Eligibility Enquiry. The Ministry will confirm whether a ban applies. Reasons are not disclosed to the individual on grounds of privacy and national security. Any government or recognised authority that requires disclosure of the reasons for a citizenship ban may make a formal written request to the Ministry; such requests will be considered on a case-by-case basis in accordance with Article X of the Constitution and applicable data protection law.

1

Send an email to office@labradoria.org

Use the subject line Citizenship Application. Include your full name, country of residence, and a brief statement confirming that you affirm the Constitution and values of the Republic. You can also use the contact form above.

2

Pay the £5 application fee

A non-refundable processing fee of £5 is required with your application. Payment details will be provided upon receipt of your email. The fee covers the administrative cost of processing, reviewing, and registering your application. It is not refunded if the application is unsuccessful.

3

Await confirmation

Once your application and fee are received, the Ministry of Internal Affairs & Citizenship will review your application. You will receive a formal confirmation of citizenship — or, if unsuccessful, a written explanation — within 14 business days.

Summary: Email office@labradoria.org · Subject: Citizenship Application · Pay £5 · Await confirmation within 14 business days.

🏠 Payment Information

All fees are paid to an official Labradorian bank account. Full bank details will be provided by the Ministry by email after you submit your application. When making any payment, you must include the correct payment reference so the Republic can identify your payment and link it to your application.

Payment reference format:

LABRADORIA-CITIZENSHIP-[YOUR FULL NAME]

Example: LABRADORIA-CITIZENSHIP-JOHN SMITH
The Ministry will guide you through the full payment process by email.
